Ultrasonography is effectively used to check for growths and foreign objects that are close to the bodyâ€™s surface, such as those in the thyroid gland, breasts, testes, and limbs, as well as some lymph nodes.

Ultrasonography is effectively used to image internal organs in the abdomen, pelvis, and chest.
